🍕 Pizza Sales Analysis Dashboard
🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes pizza sales data to uncover insights into customer preferences, sales trends, and product performance.

The goal is to help the business make data-driven decisions by identifying:

Peak sales periods

Popular pizza categories and sizes

Best and worst performing products

🎯 Business Problem

How can the company analyze pizza sales data to:

Identify sales trends over time

Understand customer preferences (size, category)

Optimize menu and inventory decisions

Improve overall sales performance

📁 Dataset Overview

The dataset contains transactional records of pizza orders, including:

🔑 Key Features:

Order Date & Time

Pizza Name

Pizza Category

Pizza Size

Quantity Sold

Total Price

🛠️ Project Workflow
1️⃣ Data Cleaning & Preparation

Handled missing/null values

Converted date & time into proper formats

Created new columns:

Day

Month

Hour

Ensured consistency in categories and sizes

2️⃣ Data Analysis (SQL)

Performed analysis to answer key business questions:

Total Orders & Revenue

Daily & Monthly sales trends

Sales distribution by category and size

Top 5 pizzas by:

Revenue

Quantity

Orders

Bottom 5 pizzas (underperformers)

3️⃣ Dashboard (Power BI)

Developed an interactive dashboard with the following insights:

📊 Key KPIs:

Total Revenue

Total Orders

Total Pizzas Sold

Average Order Value

📈 Visualizations:

Daily Trend for Total Orders (Bar Chart)

Monthly Trend for Orders (Line Chart)

Sales % by Pizza Category (Pie Chart)

Sales % by Pizza Size (Pie Chart)

Total Pizzas Sold by Category (Funnel Chart)

Top 5 Best Sellers (Bar Chart)

Bottom 5 Worst Sellers (Bar Chart)

📊 Key Insights

Certain pizza categories contribute the highest share of revenue

Medium and Large sizes dominate customer preference

Sales show clear daily and monthly patterns

A small number of pizzas generate majority of revenue (Pareto effect)

Some products consistently underperform and impact profitability

💡 Business Recommendations

🍕 Promote Best Sellers
Focus marketing campaigns on top-performing pizzas

📉 Optimize Menu
Remove or improve low-performing pizzas

🕒 Peak Time Strategy
Increase staffing and inventory during high-demand periods

🎯 Size-Based Pricing Strategy
Optimize pricing for high-demand sizes

📢 Targeted Offers
Use promotions to boost low-performing categories
